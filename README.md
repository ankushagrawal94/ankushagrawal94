# Hi, I'm Ankush

I'm a product leader and former software engineer. I spent roughly five years each at early Uber and Netflix, building consumer products, machine-learning systems, and global operational platforms.

My best work starts with a capability or problem that is still poorly understood, then turns it into a product people can use at scale. Most of that work was built inside Netflix and Uber, so this page is a product portfolio with public evidence rather than a collection of open-source code.

## Selected product work

### [Netflix Voice](https://www.theverge.com/column/925898/netflix-ai-voice-search)

**Product lead | Conversational discovery on TV**

I built Netflix's first Voice AI system end to end, spanning partnerships with TV manufacturers, API design, speech recognition, internally trained models, reasoning, and the architecture connecting those components into a consumer experience at global scale.

[See Netflix Voice in The Verge →](https://www.theverge.com/column/925898/netflix-ai-voice-search)

### [Netflix TV experience](https://about.netflix.com/en/news/unveiling-our-innovative-new-tv-experience)

**Product lead | Evidence and title evaluation**

I led the evidence layer for Netflix's redesigned TV experience, including how information, recommendations, autoplay, evidence, and layout help hundreds of millions of members evaluate a title and decide what to watch.

[See Netflix's redesigned TV experience →](https://about.netflix.com/en/news/unveiling-our-innovative-new-tv-experience)

### [Muse and Creative Innovation](https://netflixtechblog.com/scaling-muse-how-netflix-powers-data-driven-creative-insights-at-trillion-row-scale-aa9ad326fd77)

**Founder and product lead | Machine-learning products for creative teams**

I founded the Creative Innovation work that became Muse, an internal product that gives creative teams data-driven insight into how artwork and promotional video perform. Muse's impression and playback datasets grew to the order of trillions of rows a year.

[Read how Netflix scaled Muse →](https://netflixtechblog.com/scaling-muse-how-netflix-powers-data-driven-creative-insights-at-trillion-row-scale-aa9ad326fd77)

### [Uber Real-Time Document Check](https://www.uber.com/us/en/blog/ubers-real-time-document-check/)

**Product lead | Global onboarding and identity infrastructure**

I owned Uber's onboarding and identity platform across cities and work types. I led document-processing systems handling more than 10 million driver and rider documents each month, including real-time classification, transcription, fraud detection, configurable verification, and human review.

[Read about Uber's Real-Time Document Check →](https://www.uber.com/us/en/blog/ubers-real-time-document-check/)
